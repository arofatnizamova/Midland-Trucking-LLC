About us

History and
development

The company was founded in 2020 and has demonstrated stable development from the very beginning

Initially, the company followed a different strategy, but over time it focused on acquiring its own fleet

This step was a logical continuation of the opening of a service center in Florida and obtaining licenses to participate in auctions

Throughout our journey, we have faced several challenging periods that tested our resilience.

Each time, our team worked together to find effective solutions, gaining invaluable experience that continues to shape our approach today.

These difficulties strengthened our organization and motivated us to keep improving, preparing us for future challenges

What began as a small operation with a fleet of just five trucks has grown into a company with a comprehensive, end-to-end logistics ecosystem.

We remain committed to continuous improvement, operational excellence, and delivering high-quality service as we move forward on our path of growth and innovation

Strategy and

Today, we actively invest in modern equipment, advanced technologies, and strategic facilities that enhance both our performance and the quality of service we deliver

The development of our service center in Florida marked a major step toward becoming more self-sufficient, allowing us to maintain our fleet at the highest standards and reduce operational downtime
